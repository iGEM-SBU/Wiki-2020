Meet the Team


Introducing Stony Brook 2020 iGEM team! Stony Brook iGEM was established in 2014, making us the 7th team to compete! Throughout the years, the team has found several
successes and we aim to continue that! This year brings a variety of disciplines across the spectrum together in order to tackle a real-world challenge.
Starting from February, we worked together as a team despite our differences and the challenges, especially that of COVID-19, in order to create a solution that each one of us is proud of.
